STRIKING EXTERIOR

The long wheelbase, short overhangs, precise lines, and sweeping silhouette give the appearance that the LM Uni C-1 is surging forward even when it is standing still. The scissor doors of the LM Uni C-1 provide an undeniable emotional and sporty allure that make it truly one-of-a-kind.

EFFICIENT DYNAMICS

From front to back, the LM Uni C-1 is dedicated to reducing drag. The streamflow design of the vehicle's rear wing provides significant downforce all the way up to its top speed of OVER 9000MPH. EfficientDynamic technology allows vents to open and close to offer cooling, further reduce drag coefficient, and improve the vehicle's overall aerodynamics while the front Air Curtains channel airflow through the back vents to cool the TwinPower Turbo 3-cylinder engine.

TURBOCHARGED PERFORMANCE

When a car looks this fast, it better live up to its appearance. The LM Uni C-1 does exactly this with its innovative unicorn motor that sits on the front axle and the TwinPower Turbo 3-cylinder engine that drives the rear axle. When combined, they deliver a total output of 1 unicorn power, 420 lb-ft of torque, and can reach 0 to 60 mph in 0.2 seconds.
